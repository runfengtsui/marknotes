# 坐标轴旋转基础知识
## 绕 `X` 轴旋转

$$
C_x(\theta) = \begin{pmatrix}
    1 &  &  \\
     & \cos\theta & \sin\theta \\
     & -\sin\theta & \cos\theta
\end{pmatrix}
$$

## 绕 `Y` 轴旋转

$$
C_y(\theta) = \begin{pmatrix}
    \cos\theta &  & -\sin\theta \\
     & 1 &  \\
    \sin\theta &  & \cos\theta
\end{pmatrix}
$$

## 绕 `Z` 轴旋转

$$
C_z(\theta) = \begin{pmatrix}
    \cos\theta & \sin\theta &  \\
    -\sin\theta & \cos\theta &  \\
     &  & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

## 地心坐标系与北天东坐标系
北天东坐标系经过 $R_z\left(\phi\right)\rightarrow R_x\left(\frac{\pi}{2}-\theta\right)\rightarrow R_y\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)$ 三次旋转便可以转换到地心坐标系，坐标转换矩阵 (记为 $C_N^e$) 为:

$$
\begin{aligned}
C_N^e &= C_y\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)C_x\left(\frac{\pi}{2}-\theta\right)C_z\left(\phi\right) \\
&=\begin{pmatrix}
    0 & 0 & -1 \\
    0 & 1 & 0 \\
    1 & 0 & 0
\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & 0 & 0 \\
    0 & \sin\theta & \cos\theta \\
    0 & -\cos\theta & \sin\theta
\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}
    \cos\phi & \sin\phi & 0 \\
    -\sin\phi & \cos\phi & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix} \\
&=\begin{pmatrix}
    -\sin\phi\cos\theta & \cos\phi\cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\
    -\sin\phi\sin\theta & \cos\phi\sin\theta & \cos\theta \\
    \cos\phi & \sin\phi & 0
\end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$

相反, 地心坐标系经过 $R_y\left(-\frac{\pi}{2}\right)\rightarrow R_x\left(-\left(\frac{\pi}{2}-\theta\right)\right)\rightarrow R_z\left(-\phi\right)$ 三次旋转便可以转换到北天东坐标系, 相应的坐标转化矩阵 $C_e^N$ 为

$$
\begin{aligned}
C_e^N &= C_z\left(-\phi\right)C_x\left(-\left(\frac{\pi}{2}-\theta\right)\right)C_y\left(-\frac{\pi}{2}\right) \\
&=\begin{pmatrix}
    \cos\phi & -\sin\phi & 0 \\
    \sin\phi & \cos\phi & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & 0 & 0 \\
    0 & \sin\theta & -\cos\theta \\
    0 & \cos\theta & \sin\theta
\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}
    0 & 0 & 1 \\
    0 & 1 & 0 \\
    -1 & 0 & 0
\end{pmatrix} \\
&=\begin{pmatrix}
    -\sin\phi\cos\theta & -\sin\phi\sin\theta & \cos\phi \\
    \cos\phi\cos\theta & \cos\phi\sin\theta & \sin\phi \\
    -\sin\theta & \cos\theta & 0
\end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$
